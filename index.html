Frequently Asked Questions

How long will it take to get my art?

This will fluctuate a lot based on the number of artists and claimed rewards there are. Our goal is to have your art reward sent to you within a few weeks of you filling out the form. If feel you have been waiting a while, ask an @Admin and they can give you an updated timeframe.

Can I claim art more than once?

Our goal is to encourage people to start getting involved in activism, in hopes that they continue their involvement. Because of this, we only incentivize the first action as a "push" to get started. Please only fill out the form once.

Can I be an artist on your team?

Absolutely! We always welcome more artist volunteers. Reach out to us at contact@art4palestine.org and tell us a bit about yourself with some art samples if you're interested!
