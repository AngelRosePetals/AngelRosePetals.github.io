Fundraisers & Charities

There are a lot of fundraisers and charities out there that benefit Palestinian relief efforts. We've linked a few that we know to be reputable here.

  • Palestine Children’s Relief Fund (PCRF)

    Provides medical and humanitarian relief to children regardless of their nationality, politics or religion. Currently focusing on bringing supplies, as medical care is being restricted. Based in USA.

  • Islamic Relief USA (IRUSA)

    Provides relief through food, hygiene and medical supplies, clinics, and trauma response. Based in USA.

  • United Nations Relief and Works Agency (UNRWA)

    Provides education, health and mental health care, relief and social services, microcredit and emergency assistance in 8 Palestine refugee camps in the Gaza Strip. Based throughout Middle East.

  • Human Concern International

    Providing a 3 phase approach: 1. basic needs/healthcare 2. rebuilding housing/education 3. restoring health systems, psychosocial support, and infrastructure and social systems. Muslim organization based in Canada.

  • Medical Aid for Palestinians (MAP)

    Provides immediate medical aid to those in great need, while also developing local capacity and skills to ensure the long-term development of the Palestinian healthcare system. Based in UK.

Contacting Representatives

Contacting your representatives brings your concerns directly to those who can influence policy and make tangible changes. It's a crucial step in advocating for human rights and seeking an end to the ongoing crisis.

  • Ceasefire Today

    Information for emailing, calling, protesting, petitions, and more can be found here. This site includes information for the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada.

  • 5 Calls

    Calling your congressperson is the most effective way to influence policy. This is what inspired 5 Calls, a 501(c)4 non-profit that makes this process easier by finding your representatives' contact information and providing a script for your phone call.

  • faxZERO

    If you'd prefer to contact your representative by fax, faxZERO is a service that allows 5 free faxes a day.

Boycotting

Boycott, Divestment, Sanctions (BDS) is a Palestinian-led movement for freedom, justice and equality. They have created a boycott list as shown below:

  • Consumer boycott targets

    The BDS movement calls for a complete boycott of these brands carefully selected due to the company's proven record of complicity in Israeli apartheid.

  • Divestment targets

    The BDS movement is pressuring governments, institutions and investment funds to exclude and divest from as many complicit companies as practical, especially weapons manufacturers, banks, and companies listed in the UN database of business involved in Israel’s illegal settlement enterprise, as well as the WhoProfits and AFSC Investigate databases of companies enabling the occupation.

  • Pressure (non-boycott) targets

    The BDS movement actively calls for pressure campaigns against these brands and services due to their complicity in Israeli apartheid. We have not, on strategic grounds, called for a boycott of these brands and services, instead we strategically call on supporters and institutions to mount other forms of pressure on them until they end their complicity in Israeli apartheid.

  • Organic boycott targets

    The BDS movement did not initiate these grassroots boycott campaigns but is in support of them due to these brands openly supporting Israel’s genocide against Palestinians.
